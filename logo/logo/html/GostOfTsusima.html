Ghost of Tsushima

Ghost of Tsushima is an open-world action-adventure game developed by Sucker Punch Productions. Set in feudal Japan during the Mongol invasion, the game follows Jin Sakai, a samurai warrior, as he tries to protect his home and people. With stunning landscapes, intense combat, and a deep story, this game is a must-play for fans of action RPGs.
